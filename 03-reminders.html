Email 02 · T+24h
The question that decides whether AI helps you.
Latoya U. Robinson, M.S.
latoya@lurgrowth.com

Maya,

A quick one. Most AI integrations at nonprofits fail the same way: a tool gets adopted, a workflow never gets designed, and three months in everyone quietly goes back to the way they were doing things before.

The question that splits the two outcomes is deceptively plain:

Is this workflow freeing us to do better work, or is it diluting what made the work ours?

On Thursday, I'll show you where this question belongs at each layer (Comms, Ops, Programs) and what to do when the honest answer is "diluting." It's not always "stop." Sometimes it's "redesign." But it's never "keep going and hope."

See you Thursday at noon ET.

, L.
